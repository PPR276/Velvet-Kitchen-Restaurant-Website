GARLIC NAAN

Garlic Naan is a soft and fluffy Indian flatbread infused with garlic and butter, typically cooked in a tandoor (clay oven). Made from all-purpose flour, yogurt, and yeast, it has a slightly chewy texture with a golden, crispy exterior.Topped with chopped garlic and coriander, it pairs perfectly with rich curries and gravies.
